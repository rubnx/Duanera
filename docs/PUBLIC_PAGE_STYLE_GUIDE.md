# Public Page Style Guide

## Purpose

This document governs future public pages: landing page, pricing, about, legal, support, educational content, and SEO pages.

Public pages are not the priority until the product/data foundation is clearer.

---

## Current certainty level

### Confirmed

- Duanera should sound serious, clear, and credible.
- Avoid hype.
- Avoid overclaiming before data coverage is known.
- Spanish-first is likely for initial public pages.

### Unknown

- Final target segment.
- Pricing model.
- Data coverage claims.
- Legal/licensing claims.
- Brand voice beyond early direction.

---

## Voice

Use a restrained, professional tone.

Duanera should not sound like:

- a crypto dashboard
- a generic startup
- a magical AI tool
- a noisy lead-gen site

It should sound like a serious trade-data product.

---

## Claim discipline

Do not claim:

- complete coverage
- official status
- guaranteed accuracy
- all import/export activity
- real-time data
- global coverage
- exclusive intelligence

unless verified and documented.

Use careful phrasing:

- “datos organizados”
- “fuentes disponibles”
- “registros procesados”
- “cobertura inicial”
- “según la fuente disponible”
- “cuando el dato está disponible”

---

## Public page priorities

Later pages may include:

- Landing
- Pricing
- Data coverage
- Methodology
- Contact
- Legal notice
- Privacy policy
- Terms
- Blog / guides

The “Methodology” or “Data coverage” page may become especially important for trust.

---

## SEO content direction

Potential content areas:

- cómo buscar importadores en Chile
- cómo analizar importaciones por partida arancelaria
- qué es el código HS
- importaciones por país de origen
- análisis de competidores por importación
- cómo usar datos de aduanas para prospección comercial

Do not publish thin SEO content before data methodology is credible.
